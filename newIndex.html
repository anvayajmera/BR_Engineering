About Us

Hello BR Engineers,

I am honored to be the new president of BR Engineering Club! We have a thrilling year ahead with numerous activities and opportunities for growth and learning. Your involvement is crucial, and we look forward to your contributions.

Lessons: Our lessons will cater to various skill levels, from beginner to intermediate. The goal is to prepare members for hands-on projects and upcoming competitions. Expect engaging sessions that enhance your engineering skills and knowledge.

Projects: Hands-on experience is vital for learning engineering. We will host diverse projects throughout the year. If you are interested in more advanced projects, there may be a small fee associated. Get ready to roll up your sleeves and dive into exciting projects!

Competition: We are gearing up for the Terra North Jersey STEM Fair (TNJSF). Your commitment and participation will determine your eligibility for this prestigious competition. Make sure to stay engaged and contribute actively to the club.

Officer Applications: Leadership is key to a successful club. We are seeking members to fill officer roles such as Coordinator, Treasurer, and Publicist. If you're interested, apply through the Google Form linked below.

For any questions or suggestions, feel free to email me at contact@brengineering.com.

Our Projects

Project 1: Solar Fans

This project focuses on designing and building solar-powered fans to provide relief in hot climates. The fans are aimed at helping communities in India and Africa.

Project 2: RC Boats

Our RC boat project involves creating remote-controlled boats using Arduino and 3D-printed parts. It's a fun way to learn about electronics and mechanical design.

Project 3: iPhone Prototypes

In this project, we experiment with designing iPhone prototypes, exploring various features and functionalities. It's an exciting way to delve into product design and development.
